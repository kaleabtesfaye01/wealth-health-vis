Global Health vs Wealth

How does economic prosperity influence the physical well-being of a nation? This dashboard provides a multi-dimensional analysis of global progress, mapping the correlation between wealth metrics like GDP and critical health outcomes.

How to Explore:

  • Interactive Brushing: Draw a selection on the map or any chart to highlight specific countries. If any part of a country falls within your selection, it will be included.
  • Linked Highlighting: Watch how your selection on one chart illuminates corresponding data points across all others.
  • Preserved Context: Unselected data remains visible as a "ghost" background, allowing you to compare your subset against the global whole.
